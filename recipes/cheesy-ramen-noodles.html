Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Description

Melting cheese in ramen makes for an extremely simple and delicious spin on typical ramen noodles that can be easily made in the college environment.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of watter
  • 11 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American Cheese

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  2. Pour out water.
  3. Stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
